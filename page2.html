Underground Appp was designed to help locals find events within thier area. Users are able to find activities such as food trucks, concerts and much more. This was a team based project and was developed using data gathered from users. We conducted surveys, interviews, and conductred Guerilla Tests to find how effective this app was. We did trial and error and eventually decided on a color scheme that worked best.

The Problem: People are unable to find local events based on their likes and interests.

Solution: Undergound App tells the user about small events in thier area.
